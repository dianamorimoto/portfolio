About Me

Next semester I will be taking CS 111! (I am very excited for the class.)

As you can probably tell, Taylor Swift is one of my favorite favorite music artists. I love listening to mainstream pop artists such as Sabrina Carpenter and Olivia Rodrigo. In my free time, I love to read. I read a wide range of books from contemporary romance to fantasy to women's studies and sociology. Some of my favorite authors include India Holton, Chloe Gong, and Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie. The last book I read was "What My Bones Know" by Stephanie Foo.
